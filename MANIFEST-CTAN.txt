Summary:
LaTeX thesis template for Chongqing Jiaotong University (CQJTU)

Description:
cqjtuthesis is a LaTeX document class for typesetting theses at Chongqing 
Jiaotong University. It supports Bachelor's thesis, Academic Master's thesis,
Professional Master's thesis, and Doctoral dissertation. The template strictly
follows the official formatting requirements from the university.
